What's Gojabz about ?

Gojabz is all about community. Find new friends, allies or future colleges.

What's it's not

Gojabz is not Facebook. It won't help you push your ego or promote yourself.

Gojabz won't sell your data or use it against you.

How does it work ?

All users pay for the service and thus are our first class customers. You decide what Gojabz will be.

You set your goals and what you want to do with Gojabz and Gojabz will help you to run your project.
